Help & Tips

⚡

Quick Start Rounds

  • Start a round instantly with all holes set to Par 4.
  • Choose either 9 holes or 18 holes.
  • Quick Start is available on both iPhone and Apple Watch.
  • Perfect for casual rounds or when you don't want to enter course details.
🏁

Starting a Round

  • Tap Start Round on the home screen.
  • Enter the course name and choose 9 or 18 holes.
  • Your round saves automatically as you play.
📋

Managing Rounds

  • From the home screen, manage any saved round.
  • Swipe right to show the Copy and Rename buttons.
  • Swipe left to show the Delete button.
  • Copying creates a new round with the same setup.
  • Deleting always shows a confirmation first.
✏️

Tracking Your Score

  • Strokes are full swings before the green.
  • Putts are strokes taken on the green.
  • Penalties include lost balls, OB, water hazards, etc.
  • You can change any score at any time.
⌚

Using the Apple Watch

  • Swipe left or right to move between holes.
  • Tap +/– to update strokes, putts, or penalties.
  • Once you add your first putt, strokes lock to prevent mistakes.
  • Your watch and phone stay in sync as you play.
🔄

How Syncing Works

  • Your iPhone and Apple Watch update each other automatically.
  • Whichever device you used most recently updates the other.
  • If something looks out of sync, opening the iPhone app usually fixes it.
  • New rounds may take a moment to appear on the watch.
🕐

Editing Previous Holes

  • Go back and fix any hole at any time.
  • Helpful if you forgot a penalty or mis‑counted.
  • The watch may show a small reminder if you're editing an older hole.
✅

Finishing a Round

  • A round is complete once all holes have scores.
  • You can still make changes after finishing.
  • Your rounds appear in the history on the home screen.
⚠️

Troubleshooting

  • If the watch doesn't update, open the iPhone app.
  • Make sure Bluetooth is turned on.
  • Restarting the watch app can help if things get stuck.
  • If the watch shows No Active Round, start a round on your iPhone.
  • If you changed phones or watches, tap Restore Purchase in Settings.
🎙️

Using Siri

  • "Add a stroke" — increments strokes on the current hole.
  • "Add a putt" — increments putts on the current hole.
  • "Add a penalty" — increments penalties on the current hole.
  • "Next hole" — advances to the next hole and syncs to Watch.
  • "What hole am I on?" — reports hole number, strokes, and score term.
  • "What's my round score?" — reports gross total and over/under par.
  • "What's my hole score?" — reports the current hole using golf terms (Eagle, Birdie, Bogey, etc.).
  • Works on iPhone and Apple Watch · Requires active Pro trial or purchase.
