Golf Caddie — Score Tracker

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026 · iOS & Apple Watch

The short version: Golf Caddie does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Your scorecard data lives in your iCloud account and is never seen by us.

Data We Collect

None. Golf Caddie does not collect any personal information, usage data, analytics, or crash reports.

Your Scorecard Data

All round and scorecard data you enter is stored exclusively in your personal iCloud account via Apple's CloudKit. This data is:

  • Stored in your private iCloud database
  • Synced between your iPhone and Apple Watch via iCloud
  • Never accessible to us or any third party
  • Deleted when you delete your iCloud data or uninstall the app

In-App Purchases

Golf Caddie Pro is a one-time purchase processed entirely by Apple. We never see your payment information. Purchase and subscription status is managed by Apple's StoreKit and is stored locally on your device.

Third-Party Services

Golf Caddie uses the following Apple services:

  • iCloud / CloudKit — for data sync across your devices
  • StoreKit — for processing the Pro upgrade
  • WatchConnectivity — for communicating between iPhone and Apple Watch

These are governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

Golf Caddie does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new date.
